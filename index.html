DUNGEONS & DRAGONS:
What you need for your first game.

THE GAME

Dungeons & Dragons, at its core, is all about story telling. Together, all players tell a story under the guidance of the Dungeon Master. Adventuring through quests, battles, daring rescues, and more, players and the DM create an immersive world filled with wonder and intrigue.

Things you will need to start playing include:

  • 4-5 Players and 1 Dungeon Master
  • The core set of rules
  • Some Polyhedral Dice
  • Your Imaginations!

STARTER SET

The starter set contains everything you need to start playing the world's greatest roleplaying game.
Designed for up to 5 players and 1 Dungeon Master, you can explore subterranean labyrinths, plunder for treasure, and battle fearsome monsters!

  • 64-page adventure book
  • 32-page rulebook
  • 5 pre-generated characters
  • 6 dice

Join the adventure! The Stater Set is perfect for jumping into the realms of D&D!

https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0786965592

PLAYERS HAND BOOK

Create heroic characters; Brave Fighters, Devoted Clerics, Fearsome Barbarians, Cunning Rogues, Learned Wizards, & more.

The Player's Handbook is an essential reference for all D&D roleplayers. It provides rules for the creation of your own character, to give them backgrounds and skills, equipment and spells, as well as other features.

Bring together your characters to adventure into the world of D&D. Battle great evils, quest for fantastic treasures, gain experience and power as you and your companions make your place in the world.

https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0786966629

DUNGEON MASTERS GUIDE

The Dungeon Master leads the adventuring party through the world you create together.

Giving voice to the world and its inhabitants the Dungeon Master is key in shaping the world you will all forge. With this in mind the Dungeon Master's Guide is an amazing tool, providing inspiration and guidance to spark your imagination.

Inside there are tools to help build your world. Tips and tricks for creating memorable locations and characters. Rules to help your players enjoy their experience, and much much more.

https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0786966629

MONSTER MANUAL

The world is full of deadly monsters and foul beasts, some will assault you physically whilst other will toy with your mind!

The Monster Manual gives a menagerie of classic D&D creatures, from Dragons to Beholders. This book is a great tool for Dungeon Masters to expand their world and create unique challenges for players.

The creature contained in these pages are straight from D&D's history. There are easy-to-use stat blocks for every creature you may want your party to encounter. As well as interesting stories and descriptions to help you picture the beasts you will face.

https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0786966629

EXPANDING YOUR GAME

Enjoying your gaming experience? Got a taste fot the adventuring/ story telling life?
Expand your world further! There are a large range of adventure books and additional resources to dive into.
Adventure into new parts of the D&D world, discover new spells, branch out with new class backgrounds and features, and more!

Enjoy your gaming and have fun with the endless possibilities of D&D. Remember that you are all telling the story together so work together with your fellow players and your Dungeon Master to make a memorable adventure.
